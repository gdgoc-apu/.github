# Code of Conduct

GDGoC APU is committed to providing a respectful, inclusive, and beginner-friendly environment. This applies to our GitHub organisation, issues, pull requests, discussions, events, workshops, and community spaces.

## Expected behaviour

Community members are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive
- Welcome beginners and new contributors
- Give feedback politely
- Credit others' work properly
- Communicate clearly
- Focus on helping the project and community improve

## Unacceptable behaviour

The following behaviour is not accepted:

- Harassment, bullying, or personal attacks
- Discrimination or exclusionary comments
- Insults, threats, or intimidation
- Repeated disruptive behaviour
- Sharing private information without permission
- Plagiarism or claiming others' work as your own

## Reporting

If you notice behaviour that goes against this Code of Conduct, please contact the project maintainers or GDGoC APU leadership privately at **gdgoc.apu@gmail.com**.

Reports will be reviewed respectfully and handled with care.

## Consequences

Maintainers may take action when needed, including warnings, removing comments, closing issues, restricting participation, or removing access from the organisation.

## Our goal

We want GDGoC APU to be a safe and productive space where students can learn, build, and grow together.
